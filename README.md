📊 Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard (2022 to 2024)
Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive sales analysis of over 70,000 sales records spanning 2022, 2023, and 2024. The objective was to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that support strategic decision-making.
Using Microsoft Excel, the dataset was cleaned, analyzed, and visualized through an interactive dashboard, enabling users to monitor sales performance, identify trends, and evaluate key business metrics.
Objectives:

The project was designed to answer the following business questions:
How has revenue changed across the three-year period?
Which products and product categories generate the highest sales?
What are the monthly sales trends?
How accurate are the sales forecasts?
What factors may have contributed to increases or decreases in revenue?
What recommendations can improve future sales performance?

Key Insights

The analysis revealed several important business insights, including:

Revenue patterns across multiple years.
Peak and low-performing sales periods.
Products consistently driving business growth.
Product categories with the highest contribution to revenue.
Variations between forecasted and actual sales performance.
Potential reasons behind sales increases and declines.
Business Recommendations

Based on the findings, an executive report was prepared with recommendations such as:

Increase investment in high-performing products and categories.
Develop promotional strategies during historically low-performing months.
Improve inventory planning using historical sales trends.
Refine forecasting models to enhance planning accuracy.
Focus marketing efforts on products demonstrating sustained growth.
